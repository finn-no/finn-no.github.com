Open Source at FINN.no

We love open source. In fact, all of our products are built using vast amounts of open source technology. Therefore, it is only fitting that we try to give back by open-sourcing some of the things we're building ourselves.

Open source is all about working together, and we'd love to receive any of your comments, issues or pull requests for the projects we have made available!

In the spirit of open source, we have also made our Open Source Policy available online.
